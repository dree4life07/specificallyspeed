ABOUT SPEED

Speed provides developmental systems for high-performance athletes. Our programs are designed to improve athletic performance with a focus on speed, biomechanics, and sports specific training.

We have an extensive track record in working with young prepubescent athletes, all the way up to NFL Pro athletes. We prepare players for the NFL Combine and Pro-Days, as well as many Olympic caliber athletes in other fields.

IT'S ALL IN THE NUMBERS

How can you make an assessment and effect a performance change without baseline information? We’re not talking about measuring a 40 with a stopwatch. We’re talking about true measurements of speed and change of direction. Measurements of stride length, stride frequency, meters per second, ground contact time, rate of acceleration, and deceleration are all critical.

How do you effect a physiological change when you only have the player for 6, 8, or 12 weeks? Others might say it’s no problem, but it is a challenge. Through S.P.E.E.D., we effect change in the shortest time possible. Our metrics remove the subjectivity from development of human performance. We can demonstrate actual results because we perform initial testing to establish baseline measures and perform additional testing throughout the training period as well as the end, so that change is measured.
