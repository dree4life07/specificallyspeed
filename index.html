Process

Let's Chat

Once initial contact has been made, we can chat via email, phone, or zoom about your business and what you’re looking for from a designer. In turn, I will offer any insight and initial comments before we proceed.

Planning

When we have agreed upon a package specifically tailored to your needs, I’ll get to work on a strategy and a few designs for you to consider. It’s about colors, style, typefaces and bringing your brand to life.

Design

Once you are happy with the direction we are taking, the fun begins. I will curate the concepts we previously discussed into a unique design made for you or your business and walk you through my thought process.

Final Approval

Upon approval, I will work on the finishing touches, create usable, real-life versions of our concept to fit your needs and send you any and all content related to the project to use as you see fit!
